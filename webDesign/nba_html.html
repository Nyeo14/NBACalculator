METHODOLOGY

The main objective of this project was to predict the winner of games from the 2018-2019 Nba season according to our standards. Using Python and the Requests library the group sourced data from the Balldontlie API, containing player information including their team, field goal attempts, field goals made, and many other statistics relevant to basketball. Once we gathered the neccessary data, the group then asssessed the value of teams and players using our separate formulas. Possibily touch on why we did this project / reduce bias of a favoring teams becasue of a star player by analyzing just the statistics of an entire team combined to truly see what team was better.

FINDINGS

Example : After running our formulas on all 82 games played in the 2018 - 2019 Nba season, we were able to predict the outcome of games at a rate of 53.5%. When analyzing the values of each statisitci in basketball (rebounds , points, etc.) it was clear that true shooting percentages as a team correleated to higher chances of winning whereas blocks did not contribute as much success as perceived normally.

Example : Another interesting point unconvered in our analysis was that teams with high defensive efficiency or high offensive efficiency on average beat teams with normal efficiency on either the defensive or offensive ends. However when a team with high defensive efficiency went against a team with high offensive efficieny, most of the time the team with high offensive efficiency wins, further supporting the modern era of a high scoring offense in the Nba.
