2018-2019 Nba Season Calculator

Timothy Tai, Justin Lu, Nate Yeo, Cole Douglas

Our main objective in this project was to predict the outcome of games during the 2018-2019 Nba season. Using the Balldontlie API we pulled the previous season averages in order to perform our calculations. The API provided player information including their team, statistics in all cateogoriesrelevant to baskebtall( Eg: Rebounds) as well as field goal attempts, and field goals made.
